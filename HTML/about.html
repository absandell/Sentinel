About This Project
Sentinel allows the user to understand some of the major humanitarian issues that are going on around the world. Each filter found in the dropdown menu gives a visual representation of data for a specific societal issue, backed by data found in public datasets. This website helps people better choose where they dedicate time, resources, and energy in order to help people around the world.
